# Day 28 — Rebranding ExamBooster to VNAverse

**Date:** 19 July 2026

---

# 🚀 Overview

Day 28 marked a major milestone in the journey of the project.

Instead of continuing development on individual features, today was dedicated to establishing the long-term identity of the platform.

The project officially evolved from **ExamBooster** into **VNAverse (Vision Nexus Academy)**—an AI-powered learning ecosystem designed to support students beyond just exam preparation.

This day focused on branding, project positioning, repository presentation, and creating a professional foundation for future products.
