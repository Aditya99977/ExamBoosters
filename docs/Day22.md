# 🚀 Day 22 - AI Tutor UI Redesign (ExamBooster)

**Date:** 13 July 2026

---

# 📌 Overview

Day 22 was dedicated to completely redesigning the **ExamBooster AI Tutor** interface.

Instead of continuing with a dashboard-like layout, the focus shifted towards building an **AI-first experience** inspired by modern AI products such as ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor and Perplexity while maintaining ExamBooster's own branding and identity.

The goal was to prepare a clean and scalable frontend before starting AI integration on Day 23.
